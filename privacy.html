Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10.04.2026

Eloi Canals Pascual respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Strings AI MacOS application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data, account information, or usage analytics. We do not have servers to store your .xcstrings files or App Store metadata.

2. Data Processing

All file parsing and processing happen locally on your Mac. To provide translation services, the app sends the specific text strings you select to third-party AI models (DeepL).

  • This data is transmitted securely via HTTPS.
  • We do not store these strings after the translation is delivered to your device.
  • Please refer to the privacy policy of DeepL AI provider regarding how they handle API data.

3. Third-Party Services

You provide your own API key to use the translation features, and key is not stored and is never transmitted to us.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
